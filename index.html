December releases

As 2025 comes to a close, dubstep producers have dropped their final batch of releases. Whether you need last-minute ideas for a musical Christmas gift or weapons for New Year’s USB sticks, there is something for every taste. A first remarkable release was Kamoun’s psychedelic work featuring dubbed-out foley sampling. This was followed by Beatrice M’s classy debut on Tempa with three delicate wobblers, as well as Henry Greenleaf’s tribute to Croydon’s embryonic dubstep. We also can’t overlook SPE:C’s 10-year anniversary remix compilation. To round things off, we share truant’s latest release: two free-download VIPs of previously released tracks, featuring house-inflected vocals and a garage-leaning feel. ...
